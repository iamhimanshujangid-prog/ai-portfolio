Final verdict
Recommendations by team type
Best overall
Claude + ChatGPT
Use Claude for document analysis and reasoning. Use ChatGPT for creative output and coding. Together they cover 90% of business AI needs.
Best for startups
ChatGPT / Gemini
Free tiers are powerful for early-stage teams. Gemini adds significant value for teams already using Google Workspace daily.
Best for enterprise
Azure AI / Copilot
Microsoft's ecosystem dominates corporate environments. Azure AI for custom models, Copilot for Office 365 productivity at scale.
Best for regulated industries
IBM Watson
Healthcare, banking, and legal sectors benefit from Watson's on-premise deployment, auditability, and compliance-ready architecture.
